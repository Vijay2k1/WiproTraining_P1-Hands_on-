Role in India's Independence

Bose was a prominent leader of the Indian National Congress and later formed the Azad Hind Fauj (Indian National Army) to fight against British rule. His famous slogan, "Give me blood, and I will give you freedom!" inspired many.

Legacy

Subhash Chandra Bose remains an iconic figure in Indian history. His mysterious disappearance in 1945 continues to be a subject of debate.
